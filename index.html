ABOUT PAQSJIMS


PAQSJIMS is a teaching hospital, It has been rated a first-rate teaching hospital at the level of a medical center by the Department of Health. In addition to its three missions as a medical center (providing medical services, teaching and training, and engaging in research and development, PAQSJIMS normally provides the best medical care available, during wartime its main function is to mobilize and integrate medical resources in different areas. Gambat one of the eight Talukas of Khairpur District, centrally located in Sindh having dual carriage highway road and laying on main railway line and one on the left bank of river Indus with 300,000 people.

MEET OUR DIRECTOR




Dr. Rahim Bux Bhatti was born on March 01, 1946. He has been associated with the medical field for over 45 years. He got his primary education from Gambat. For his secondary education, he joined Higher Secondary School Gambat, following which he went to Liaquat Medical College, University of Sindh. After his house job he Joined Pakistan Army in 1971 and served for 03 years as Captain.Then he joined Sindh Health Department as Medical officer in 1974 and posted in Dispensary at Gamabat.
